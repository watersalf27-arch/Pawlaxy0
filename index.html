Pet Knowledge Hub

1. The Scottish Fold: Ear Mutation

Scottish Folds are famous for their unique folded ears. This is a genetic mutation affecting cartilage. Care: Clean their ears weekly to avoid wax buildup.

2. The Sphynx: Hairless Mutation

The Sphynx is naturally hairless due to a genetic occurrence. Key Fact: They need regular baths to remove oils and sunscreen in the summer.

3. The Munchkin: Short-Leg Mutation

Munchkins have very short legs caused by achondroplasia. They are energetic and playful. Health: Maintain a healthy weight to protect their spine.
